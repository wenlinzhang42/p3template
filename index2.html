What is Hacker?

Hacker is a word that describes who can break into the computer or phone security system and get data. Hacker is a mystery occupation because sometimes it is hard to find their trace. When we mention the word 'Hacker', we will think they are bad people. Because they are stealing other people's data for their purpose. However, Hackers is not only a group of people that stay at home to gather other people's privacy. Actually, there are different types of Hacker and we the example describe above is only one of the types. Usually, there are three types of Hacker, which are 'Balck Hat', 'White Hat' and 'Grey Hat' Hackers.
